Prepare Your Data
Export your attendance records from your SIS as an Excel (.xlsx) file. Must include Roll No, Name, and Present % columns.
⬆️
2
Upload & Validate
Drag and drop your .xlsx file. AMS will auto-filter students below 75%, generate an Excel export, and create individual PDF shortage letters.
📊
3
Analyze & Act
Review at-risk students, send SMS alerts to parents via Twilio, and download polished PDF or Excel reports.
📌
Required File Format
Excel (.xlsx) with: Roll No, Name, Present % (0–100 or 0–1 decimal), and subject-wise columns. Group rows indicate classes held.
🔒
Data Privacy
All uploaded records are processed locally on this server. Data is sent externally only via Twilio for SMS notifications.
⚠️
Attendance Threshold
Students with Present % below 75 are automatically flagged as at-risk and queued for SMS notification.
📞
SMS Gateway Setup
Uses Twilio. Parent phone numbers are looked up from the local students.db SQLite database by Roll No. Prefix +91 (India).
